## Full description (YouTube / landing)
A 90-second walkthrough of **VibeCurve** — the anti-flex network where presence
beats permanence.

Build a profile that's truly yours by branching through flashcards — the deeper
you share, the closer your matches. Then each day, draw its emotional curve from
morning to night. VibeCurve matches you with someone whose day felt just like
yours and drops you into a small, **anonymous, emoji-only room** — no names, no
feed, no flex — that quietly **burns at midnight**.

And here, you don't buy clout — you give **warmth**: Embers ✦ you spend on other
people's words, never on standing out. Go further with **VibeCurve+**, and know
that part of every ember lights a free path for someone who can't pay — so no one
is ever priced out.

How did today feel? Draw it → **vibecurve.vercel.app**

- ✦ **The Warmth Economy** — give warmth, not clout (Glow · Candle · Aurora)
